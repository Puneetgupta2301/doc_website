About the Doctor

A Physician Who Puts Patients First

Dr. Pradeep Agrawal is one of Jaipur's most trusted senior consultants in Internal Medicine, renowned for his compassionate care, diagnostic precision, and commitment to evidence-based medicine.

With over two decades of dedicated practice at Metro MAS Health Care & Multispeciality Hospital, Mansarovar, Dr. Agrawal has helped thousands of patients manage complex conditions including diabetes, hypertension, and chronic lifestyle diseases. His approach integrates modern diagnostics with personalized treatment plans tailored to every patient's unique health needs and lifestyle.
